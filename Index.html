Base Stations and Rovers for Positioning on the Move

GNSS receivers are the backbone of any base and rover setup. Choose from receivers designed for semi-permanent mounting or others that can mount to the top of a tripod or range pole.

Survey and construction uses

HiPer receivers are a popular choice for survey applications, including property boundaries, engineering design, and cadastral projects. These versatile tools are also often used for construction site layout/stakeout, grade checking, and material volume calculations. HiPer receivers can also be paired with robotic total stations for Hybrid Positioning™.

TILT™ for hard-to-reach measurements

Measure building edges without using an offset, or stretch to otherwise out-of-reach points using Topcon Integrated Leveling Technology (TILT). This technology allows the HiPer XR, HR and VR receivers to compensate for rods that are not exactly vertical. Achieve an amazing 60 degrees tilt with the HiPer XR's new calibration-free and magnetic interference immune IMU.
